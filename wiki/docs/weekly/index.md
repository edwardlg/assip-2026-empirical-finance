# Week-by-week schedule

The eight active research weeks run **Monday June 22** through **Wednesday August 12, 2026**. Each card below is the index card for one week — open it for the Monday-through-Friday schedule, the assigned chapters and notebooks, the mentor session, and what is due Friday at 5 pm ET.

Two days are **off** for federal holiday observances: **Friday June 19** (Juneteenth — pre-program) and **Friday July 3** (Independence Day observance — Week 2). All other Mondays through Fridays are full days, 9:00 am – 5:00 pm ET.

!!! tip "How to read these cards"
    Each card lists (1) the date span, (2) the chapter range in the textbook, (3) the mentor session topic, and (4) the deliverable due that Friday. Click through for the full daily breakdown, reading list, and data-card links.

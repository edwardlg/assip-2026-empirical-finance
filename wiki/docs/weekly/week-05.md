# Week 5 — Reading the Frontier I <span class="week-badge upcoming">upcoming</span>

**July 20 – July 24, 2026** · Chapters 5.1 – 5.5 · Lab 5 · Mentor Session 5 · Problem Set 5

> One paper per day. Each Reader's Guide follows a fixed 7-part anatomy: research question · identification strategy · data · table-by-table reading order · what's clever · what's vulnerable · three replication exercises.

This is the week where the program turns from "learn the tools" to "read the literature." By Friday you will have read five top-three-journal empirical-finance papers the way a referee reads them — tables first.

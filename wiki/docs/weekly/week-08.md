## Symposium logistics

- **Date / time:** Wednesday, August 12, 2026 · poster session 1:00 – 4:00 pm ET.
- **Format:** In-person poster session at GMU Mason Square + virtual Zoom breakouts for remote interns. Our cohort is remote, so you will present in a virtual breakout room — but your printed poster will be hung at Mason Square by program staff and the in-person audience will rotate through.
- **Audience:** Mason faculty, ASSIP mentors, students' families, College of Science leadership, Mason News press.
- **Dress code:** Business casual on camera. Have a backup wired headset.
- **Poster spec:** 36" × 48" landscape; PDF; built from the template in [Projects → Poster template](../projects/poster-template.md).
- **Backup plan:** If your home internet drops, dial in by phone to the breakout room number circulated Monday Aug 10 and continue presenting from your phone while a sub-leader screen-shares the poster.

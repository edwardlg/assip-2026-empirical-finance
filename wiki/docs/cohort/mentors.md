# Mentors

The 14-student cohort is supported by a three-tier mentorship structure: one faculty mentor (Prof. Gao), four PhD/RA pod sub-leaders, and the ASSIP program office (Dr. Haymond Still and team). Below is who they are, what they own, and how to reach them.

---

## Faculty mentor

### Prof. Lei Gao
**Associate Professor of Finance** · Costello College of Business, George Mason University
:material-email: &nbsp; <lgao9@gmu.edu> &nbsp;·&nbsp; :material-school: &nbsp; PhD, University of Georgia &nbsp;·&nbsp; :material-domain: &nbsp; former SEC Division of Economic and Risk Analysis (DERA)

Prof. Gao is the faculty mentor for the ASSIP 2026 Empirical Finance Research Group. His research is in empirical corporate finance, banking, fair lending, and the regulatory uses of machine learning; he has testified to **Congress** as an expert witness on fair-lending policy, and his work has been cited in regulatory proceedings at the SEC and CFPB.

**Selected published research (verified anchor papers used in this cohort's reading list):**

- Gao, L., Wang, Y., & Zhao, J. (2024). *Mortgage lending and bank conflicts of interest: Evidence from the U.S. mortgage market.* *Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis.*
- Gao, L., Calluzzo, P., & Dong, G. N. (2017). *Has the financial system become safer after the crisis? The changing nature of financial-institution risk.* *Journal of Banking & Finance,* 80, 124 – 142.
- Gao, L., Dong, G. N., & Lin, J. (2019). *Securities litigation, withdrawal risk, and the financial intermediation chain.* *Journal of Banking & Finance,* 98, 31 – 50.

A full publication list is on his [GMU faculty page](https://business.gmu.edu/) and [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/) (TBD links).

**What he owns in this cohort:**

- The Wednesday full-cohort mentor session (2:00 – 3:30 pm ET).
- PAP sign-off (after the pod RA sub-leader reviews).
- Final poster approval before the August 12 symposium.
- One pod's weekly working session per week (rotation across the four pods).
- Office hours: **Friday 1:00 – 2:30 pm ET** (open Zoom room, link in `#announcements`).

**Best way to reach him:** Slack DM for anything not-urgent; email for institutional or formal matters; office hours for code or specification questions that need a whiteboard.

## Institutional contact — ASSIP Program Office

### Dr. Amanda Haymond Still
**ASSIP Program Director** · College of Science, George Mason University
:material-email: &nbsp; <cosassip@gmu.edu>

Dr. Haymond Still runs the **entire** ASSIP program — all mentor groups, not just ours — and is the contact for everything that crosses the line between *this cohort's empirical-finance work* and *the broader GMU program*. Email her (and Cc Prof. Gao) for:

- Anything to do with the **GMU College of Science credit transfer** to your home school.
- **Tuition, fees, or waivers** (Pell-eligible / reduced-lunch full waivers).
- The **August 12 symposium** logistics — printing, room assignments, family registration.
- **JSSR** submission paperwork after symposium.
- Code-of-conduct concerns that cannot be resolved at the pod or mentor level.
- Any medical, accessibility, or family-emergency accommodation.

The general program website is the official source for dates, deadlines, and policies; <cosassip@gmu.edu> is the human at the other end.

## Who to ping for what — quick map

| If you have a question about… | Ping… |
|---|---|
| Where to find a function in NumPy/pandas; a debugging stack trace | Your pod sub-leader, in Slack |
| Whether your research question is well-posed; how to specify your regression | Your pod sub-leader first, then Prof. Gao in Wednesday session |
| Whether your PAP is ready to file | Pod sub-leader first; final sign-off from Prof. Gao |
| WRDS access, GMU credentials, GitHub org invitation | Program office (<cosassip@gmu.edu>), Cc Prof. Gao |
| Poster printing, symposium logistics, JSSR submission | Program office |
| Credit transfer to your high school or undergrad institution | Program office |
| A teammate is being unkind, or the work culture feels off | Pod sub-leader first; Prof. Gao if unresolved; program office for anything that violates the [Code of Conduct](code-of-conduct.md) |
| You're going to miss a day or a deliverable | Pod sub-leader **and** Prof. Gao, before the deadline if possible |
